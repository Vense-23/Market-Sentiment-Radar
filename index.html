OpenAI is obligated to run ChatGPT on Microsoft Cloud, as long as there is capacity available. Besides AI there is still a lot of room for Cloud usage in general, so i dont see a realistic risk of these investments turning into a problem.

  • 4. SpaceX (Space Exploration Technologies Corp.)
    SpaceX Bankers Preparing for Bond Sale of at Least $20 Billion
    翻译：SpaceX的银行家们正准备发行至少200亿美元的债券。
    People are saying SpaceX is going to rip because Nasdaq-100 / QQQ index funds will be “forced to buy” after inclusion. I get the mechanical demand argument, but I think the actual required buying may be way smaller than the hype implies.
    翻译：人们说SpaceX会在纳入纳斯达克100指数后被纳斯达克100/QQQ指数基金“被迫购买”，股价会飙升。我理解机械性需求论点，但我认为实际所需的购买量可能远小于炒作所暗示的。
    The part I think people are missing is the low float treatment. If SpaceX is valued around $2.5T but only has ~4% public float, Nasdaq does not necessarily weight it as if the whole $2.5T company is investable. My understanding is the new rule effectively allows low-float companies to be counted at up to 3x float for weighting purposes.
    翻译：我认为人们忽略的部分是低流通股的处理方式。如果SpaceX估值约为2.5万亿美元，但只有大约4%的公众流通股，纳斯达克不一定会像整个2.5万亿美元的公司都可投资那样对其进行权重计算。我的理解是，新规则实际上允许低流通股公司在权重计算时最多按流通股的3倍计算。
