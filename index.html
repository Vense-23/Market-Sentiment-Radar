  • WEN (温迪)
    Wendy's is the third largest fast-food burger chain in the world with 7,251 locations globally at the end of Q1 2026. They operate primarily on a franchise model, with about ~400 company owned restaurants and ~6,850 franchises.
    翻译：截至2026年第一季度末，温迪是全球第三大快餐汉堡连锁店，拥有7251家门店。他们主要采用特许经营模式运营，拥有约400家公司直营餐厅和约6850家特许经营店。
    Overall, the business is in okay shape. While all profitability metrics fell in 2025 and are expected to again in 2026, they're still producing significant amounts of free cash flow, and revenue actually grew 3.3% in Q1.
    翻译：总的来说，业务状况尚可。尽管所有盈利指标在2025年下降，并预计在2026年再次下降，但他们仍在产生大量自由现金流，并且第一季度营收实际增长了3.3%。
    Same store sales fell slightly by ~3-4% in 2025, and are expected to be roughly flat in 2026. They have a lot of debt, the interest coverage ratio is only 2.5x. While not ideal, it's not some kind of emergency. They currently have no problem managing their debt load.
    翻译：2025年同店销售额略微下降约3-4%，预计2026年将大致持平。他们负债较多，利息覆盖率仅为2.5倍。虽然不理想，但这并非紧急情况。他们目前在管理债务方面没有问题。
