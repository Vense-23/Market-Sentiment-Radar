  • 1. AMZN (Amazon.com)
    Amazon says its Leo constellation has reached 396 satellites, enough to provide continuous service across its initial coverage latitudes and begin commercial operations.
    翻译：亚马逊表示其Leo卫星星座已达到396颗卫星，足以在其初始覆盖纬度提供持续服务并开始商业运营。
    The milestone turns Leo from a long-delayed infrastructure project into a functioning competitor to SpaceX’s Starlink, although early users should expect limited coverage and occasional interruptions.
    翻译：这一里程碑使Leo从一个长期拖延的基础设施项目转变为SpaceX星链的有效竞争对手，尽管早期用户应预料到覆盖范围有限和偶尔中断。
    Amazon ultimately plans to deploy 3,232 satellites, but it still has a massive gap to close against Starlink’s fleet of more than 10,000 operational satellites.
    翻译：亚马逊最终计划部署3,232颗卫星，但与星链超过10,000颗运行卫星的庞大舰队相比，仍有巨大差距需要弥补。
    The real battle may be less about matching Starlink immediately and more about Amazon bundling satellite connectivity into AWS, logistics, aviation, maritime, and enterprise contracts.
    翻译：真正的竞争可能不只是立即与星链匹敌，更多的是亚马逊将卫星连接服务捆绑到AWS、物流、航空、海事和企业合同中。
